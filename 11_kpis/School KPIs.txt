Example of KPIs for a school:

Academic Performance

Average test scores (85% achievement rate)
Graduation rate (95%)
College acceptance rate (80%)


Student Engagement

Attendance rate (96%)
Extracurricular participation (70% of students)
Homework completion rate (92%)


Teacher Effectiveness

Teacher retention rate (88%)
Professional development hours completed (40 hours/year)
Student-to-teacher ratio (18:1)


Financial Health

Budget adherence (within 3% of target)
Fundraising goals achieved (125% of target)
Cost per student ($9,500/year)


Parent Satisfaction

Parent survey results (4.2/5)
Parent-teacher conference attendance (85%)
Parent volunteer hours (1,200 hours/year)
